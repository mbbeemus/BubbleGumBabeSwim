Store Policies

Welcome to Bubblegumbabes! We're delighted to have you as a valued customer. Our commitment is to provide you with high-quality swimwear and exceptional service. Please take a moment to review our store policies to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Ordering & Payment

  • Payment Methods: We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. Your financial information is always kept confidential.
  • Order Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation with your order details shortly after placing your order. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder or contact our customer support.

Shipping & Delivery

  • Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within 3-4 business days; details will be provided during the checkout process.
  • Shipping Options: We offer standard shipping. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are provided at checkout.
  • International Orders: We ship internationally. Please note that customs and import duties may apply in your country, and it's your responsibility to cover these fees.

Returns & Exchanges

  • Return Policy: We want you to love your swimwear. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of receipt for a refund or exchange, subject to our Return Policy guidelines.
  • Return Process: To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer support team. All returned items must be in new, unworn condition with original tags attached.

Privacy & Security

  • Privacy: Your privacy is important to us. We use secure and encrypted methods to protect your personal information. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data and ensure a safe shopping experience.
